### Bookings Page
1. **Search Box**: Type customer name, phone, or event type
2. **Status Filter**: Select INQUIRY, TENTATIVE, CONFIRMED, etc.
3. **Hall Filter**: Filter by specific hall
4. **Sort Buttons**: Click Date, Name, or Amount to sort

## Common Questions

### Q: How do I refresh the data?
**A:** Data refreshes automatically when you:
- Switch between pages
- Focus the browser window
- Wait 30 seconds (auto-refresh)

### Q: Can I export data?
**A:** Yes! Go to Reports page and use export buttons for PDF or Excel.

### Q: How do I change a booking status?
**A:** Open the booking details and click the status badge to see available transitions.

### Q: What do the colors mean?
**A:** 
- Gray = Inquiry (just asking)
- Yellow = Tentative (considering)
- Blue = Confirmed (booked)
- Green = Completed (done)
- Red = Cancelled (not happening)

### Q: Can I undo an action?
**A:** Not currently - be careful when deleting. Coming soon!

### Q: How do I print a booking?
**A:** Open the booking and use your browser's print function (Ctrl+P).
